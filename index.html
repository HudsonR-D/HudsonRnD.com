About the Lab

Built to solve real infrastructure problems

Hudson R&D is an independent research lab focused on developing defensible, deployable infrastructure for a resource-constrained world. Our work spans atmospheric water harvesting, energy systems, and applied technology — all grounded in reproducible data and real-world operating conditions.

We pursue active patent filings, pursue SBIR/STTR alignment, and build tools that translate research into tangible products. From AWH prototypes to on-chain identity verification, everything we ship is instrumented and evidence-based.

Based in Denver, Colorado

Operating across diverse climate zones for credible field data.

Data-first methodology

Reproducible, defensible results that hold up to grant and partner scrutiny.
