What this is

HudsonR&D is coordinating a limited pilot to streamline simple, remote tax preparation using a secure client portal. Preparation is performed by a licensed Denver-based tax professional.

What qualifies as a "simple return"

This pilot is designed for returns that can be completed efficiently using professional software and a standardized checklist.

Typical eligible examples:

  • W-2 employees
  • Gig workers / 1099 income (straightforward, no complex structures)
  • Renters
  • Homeowners with common forms (e.g., mortgage interest) when it stays straightforward
  • Standard credits and routine documentation

What's usually NOT included (may require upgrade)

If your situation requires deeper review, additional forms, or more time, it may fall outside the simple-return pilot.

Common upgrade triggers:

  • Complex itemized deductions or extensive write-offs
  • Multiple states with complexity beyond standard handling
  • Business entities (LLCs with multi-member filings, S-Corp, etc.)
  • Significant investment activity (active trading, complicated brokerage reporting)
  • Crypto reporting that requires specialized reconciliation
  • Rental property schedules and more complex real estate scenarios
  • Anything that requires manual reconstruction due to missing documentation

How it works

  1. Pay to reserve your spot ($99 through Jan 31, then $125)

Security & privacy

  • Document upload and intake run through a secure, encrypted client portal
  • We do not ask for bank account details during reservation
  • No advertising retargeting, and no selling client data as part of this pilot
